Our Services

With Smart Solution, your dream of studying in Russia becomes a reality. We start by helping you choose the educational program that perfectly matches your goals, passions, and career aspirations, ensuring every step aligns with your future ambitions.

Once your program is selected, we guide you through the preparation and submission of all required documents. Our office has its own professional translator, who handles all academic paperwork and notary requirements, ensuring that every document is accurate, legally valid, and ready for submission.

We also assist you in obtaining your official study invitation and take care of your student visa application for the entire duration of your studies. This means you don’t have to worry about renewals, extensions, or any administrative issues—you can focus entirely on your education and personal growth.

Finally, when it’s time to begin your journey, we ensure a smooth, safe, and welcoming transition. From the moment you arrive in Russia to the first day of your classes, we are with you every step of the way.

With Smart Solution, you are never alone. We handle the paperwork, translation, notary processes, and visa management so you can concentrate on what truly matters—your studies and your future. Your journey starts here, and your success is our priority.
